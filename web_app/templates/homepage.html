About

My name is Amksa Abdelaaziz and I am and my partner Ottman Chouqar are the creators of E-Lyrics!, I am a developer turned software engineer. As a developer, I found that music was such a powerful way to help English learners to learn since they all had a natural connection to it.  I wanted to expand upon my experiences and create this educational app to help others practice their English language skills!
